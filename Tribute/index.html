Creation of the Santa-Fe Railway

  • February 11, 1859 - Chartered as the Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway
  • 1872 - Reached Wichita & Dodge City
  • 1873 - Reached Kansas-Colorado border
  • 1876 - Reached Pueblo, Colorado
  • 1878 - Battle for Raton Pass & Royal Gorge, setteled 2 years later
  • 1879 - The Atlantic and Pacific Railway (Frisco) & Santa Fe form a partnership to build west of Albuquerque
  • 1880 - Reached Albuquerque
  • 1881 - Connected with Southern Pacific at Deming, New Mexico, forming the second transcontential rail route
  • 1883 - Reached Needles, CA with a connection to Southern Pacific
  • 1885 - Grew from Barstow to San Diego
  • 1887-1888 - Expanded to Los Angeles, California; Wichita, Kansas; Fort Worth, Texas
  • 1890 - Purchace of Frisco and Colorado Midland, the system consitsed of 7,500 miles at this point
  • 1897 - Santa Fe traded the Sonora Railway of Mexico to SP for the line between Needles and Barstow, completing the link between Chicago and the Pacific Coast
  • 1899-1912 - Rapid expansion to Phoenix, the Grand Canyon, Isleta Pueblo; Brownwood
  • 1907 - Santa Fe & Southern Pacific formed Northwestern Pacific to form a route to Eureka, CA
  • 1921 - https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1d/Santa_Fe_Trail_and_Railroad_map%2C_1922.jpg
  • 1928 - sold share of NWP to SP & bought KCMO
  • December 23, 1983 - Began merger talkes with Soiuthern Pacific
  • December 31, 1996 - Merged with Burlington Northern to form BNSF
